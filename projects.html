Bahamas Feeding Network International (BFNI)

Jan 2025 - May 2025

BFNI is a not-for-profit dedicated to supporting Bahamian organizations fighting against hunger. To advance their mission, BFNI is expanding fundraising efforts beyond the Bahamas through an online grassroots campaign.

As part of my final BI teams course, I co-led a team of students providing innovation consulting services to BFNI. Our team was brought in to help BFNI establish the digital infrastructure they need to execute on their fundraising strategy. We overhauled the BFNI Website, implementing an embedded donation system and streamlining the website content. We also set up social media accounts and developed recommendations for how BFNI could use the new infrastructure to support their goals.

After our final presentation, the chairman of BFNI, Bernard Gay, said to me and my co-lead "if you represent what leadership looks like coming out of this class, you guys are going in a great direction".

AAA Medicaid Consulting

Jan 2024 - May 2024

AAA Medicaid Consulting is a local Colorado Springs small business dedicated to providing personalized Medicaid consulting services that help citizens get the most out of Medicaid. AAA Medicaid Consulting was seeking investment in order to expand their operations.

I was a member of the BI innovation consulting team that worked with AAA Medicaid to help them grow. Our team's main focus was creating data-backed visual resources that communicated the value of AAA Medicaid's services. We delivered a professionally designed PowerPoint and trifold pamphlet that AAA Medicaid could utilize in meetings with investors and potential clients.
